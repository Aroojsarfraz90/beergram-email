🔴 Key Reasons for Closures in 2025

⚔️

Intense Competition & Market Saturation: An oversaturated market makes it nearly impossible for smaller breweries to find a foothold.

Decreased Taproom Traffic: Reduced foot traffic makes it harder for smaller taproom-focused breweries to remain profitable.
